About Our Debate Club

Welcome to the CCN-UST Debate Club, a community of passionate thinkers, confident speakers, and future leaders. Our club provides a platform where students can express their ideas, challenge opinions, and develop the art of persuasive communication.

Our Mission

To empower students with strong critical thinking, public speaking, and leadership skills through debate, discussion, and teamwork.

Our Activities

  • Weekly debate practice sessions
  • Workshops on public speaking and logic
  • Inter-university debate competitions
  • Annual Debate Fest and cultural events

Our Achievements

Our club has proudly represented the university in many national debate tournaments and secured top positions.

Why Join Us?

  • Build confidence in speaking and presenting ideas.
  • Learn to research, reason, and argue respectfully.
  • Connect with like-minded students and make lasting friendships.

Our Vision

To create a culture of open-minded dialogue where every voice matters, and every opinion leads to learning.
